About

Applied ML, systems thinking, and strong product instincts.

My work sits at the boundary between modeling and infrastructure. I build systems that turn weak, delayed, or noisy signals into decisions that can be deployed safely at scale.

That has meant architecting sub-millisecond retrieval features at Meta, optimizing large NLP and LLM pipelines at Amazon Ads, and building real-time backend systems for live-streaming at TikTok.

I care about offline-online alignment, observability, latency, and failure analysis as much as model quality. The best ML systems are usually the ones with the fewest hidden assumptions.
